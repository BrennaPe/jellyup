About Us

Welcome to Jelly Up, where art meets dessert in the most delightful way!

We are a Polish couple living in Galway with a passion for crafting unique, handcrafted jelly cakes. Each of our creations is a luxurious treat, featuring intricate 3D flowers delicately suspended in a clear, flavorful jelly. Every cake is made with love, precision, and an eye for beauty—turning desserts into edible works of art.

Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in something extraordinary, our handcrafted jelly cakes bring elegance and sweetness to every moment.
